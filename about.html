About Us

Welcome to Kalna Polytechnic

Kalna Polytechnic currently offers 3-years Diploma courses in various fields of engineering and technology. The Institute is situated at Kalna Shyamganjpara . Many technical institutes of this type have been established in the state during the last five years, but these are yet to provide the gamut of quality technological professionals that is required to ameliorate West Bengal Out of the 140 Polytechnic Institutes that are currently in the state, of only one in the Sub divission Kalna of Purba Bardhaman . We have a dream project in our mind to setup an international standard full-fledged technical institute. We have no doubt about our credibility, capability or intention to conceive, construct and deliver such an Institute of high academic purpose.
